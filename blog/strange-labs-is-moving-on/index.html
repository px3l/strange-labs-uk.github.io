Strange Labs is moving on...
Monday, December 16, 2019
Connor Alexander, owner

Strange Labs is moving on

It is after great deliberation that Strange Labs is moving on. The shop is closing down as we restructure and narrow our offerings from general PC and Apple repair/refurbishing to bespoke custom PC building and website development.

We will no longer be operating from our Bristol high street store, which we will be very sadly closing from the end of March 2020.

We have had three very good years, successfully building our reputation as the top rated computer repair specialists in all of Bristol. We cannot thank our customers enough for their support, reviews and helping to spread the word and build up the Strange Labs name.

Computer Right to Strange Labs

I took over a shop called Computer Right in April 2017, seeing an opportunity to develop my technical repair skills and help the local area with their computer problems as a small business. I could see potential in Computer Right to provide the space needed for my own brand idea, Strange Labs, and spent a year redesigning the space and pushing the boundaries of what a computer repair shop could be.

I had envisioned a place on the high street where computer enthusiasts and programmers could meet to dream up ideas and learn from one another. A computer shop which offered the same service as normal, but looked and felt better in every way. One where customers would feel like they had learnt a little more about the computers they use and rely on every day.

We hosted Meetups for people interested in learning and debate around emerging technology, built some one of a kind custom PCs, provided space for technical demos using the latest hardware all the while maintaining our general repairs, help and consultation services for every type of computer problem.

Custom PCs and Software Development

The time has come however for me to focus on developing my skills further and continuing down the journey of emerging technology. I do not want to compromise the Strange Labs name on quality or price, so going down a route of a manager is not an option to me- I have no interest in training employees and moving to a management role. Although I love the satisfaction of fixing problems and helping customers, I need to carry on learning myself to stay interested and so am taking my favourite parts of what Strange Labs offers (web application and brand design, and bespoke PC builds) and focusing on those.

The high street shop has become surplus to requirements and so our last day taking jobs will be the 18th May, and our last day open will be March 20th 2020. The company Computer Right Ltd will cease trading and be wound up as the next phase of Strange Labs will not be retail. Up until that point, we will be operating as normal.

Once again, we thank all our customers for their support and hope the 2020’s will be a decade with less computer problems for them!
