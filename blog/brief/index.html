Emperors New Clothes Design Brief
Strange Labs

Project Overview

We are looking to develop a consistent and recognizable brand style for Futureproof’s educational and art content. Our aim is to create a visual identity that unifies our work across various platforms, similar to the distinctive styles of Kurzgesagt, CGP Grey, and RSA Animates. We require a collection of SVG assets that can be used on our website, in videos, and in slideshows.

Precendence and Style Guide

Kurzgesagt

Style: Visually engaging with a vibrant color palette.

Content: Educational science-based videos.

Technique: Uses an animal (bird) to represent the viewer, ensuring inclusivity across sex, gender, age, and race.

CGP Grey

Style: Distinct, clear, and simple.

Content: Educational videos and vlogs on politics and society.

RSA Animates

Style: Clean and coherent.

Content: Simplifies complex ideas often taken from talks.

Requirements

Animal Representation: Create an animal character to represent the viewer, similar to the bird used by Kurzgesagt. This character should be designed to ensure inclusivity across sex, gender, age, and race.

Color Palette: Develop a vibrant and engaging color palette that is cohesive across all assets. The palette should be versatile enough for use in various educational and artistic contexts.

Style Consistency: Ensure the design elements are consistent and recognizable, allowing our audience to easily identify Futureproof’s content.

SVG Assets: Create a collection of scalable vector graphic (SVG) assets that can be easily integrated into our website, videos, and slideshows.

Flexible Composition: Design assets that can be composed together in different formats, ensuring flexibility for various use cases.

Deliverables

Character Design: An inclusive animal character that represents the viewer.

Color Palette: A detailed color scheme to be used across all assets.

SVG Assets Collection: A comprehensive set of SVG assets that includes:

  • Icons and symbols relevant to educational and artistic content.
  • Backgrounds and patterns that can be used in various media.
  • Illustrations and graphical elements that align with the overall style.

Goals

Consistency: Create a cohesive and unified visual identity for Futureproof.

Recognition: Develop a style that is easily recognizable and stands out in the educational and art content space.

Inclusivity: Ensure the visual representation is inclusive of all demographics.
